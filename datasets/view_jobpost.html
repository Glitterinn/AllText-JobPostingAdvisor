Overview

The Caf Supervisor position drives caf sales through monitoring the productivity of the caf associates by daily dollar per person check-in goals. The Caf Supervisor is also responsible for ensuring that the caf meets every cleanliness standard, while providing a legendary customer service experience. The Caf Supervisor role achieves success through embodying our core competencies (Create the TSI Environment, Build a High Performing Team, Achieve Measurable Results).


Create the TSI Environment


Models the core attributes of a successful employee with the utmost integrity, lives the mission and empowers others to do the same; focused on building meaningful relationships with members, their kids and team members.


Responsibilities include:

  • Modeling and promoting the 5 Clubhouse Rules.
  • Being available to team members to address questions and concerns.
  • Create a strong sense of team by fostering collaboration, communication, and alignment amongst team members.
  • Adhering to all TSI policies and procedures including but not limited to timeliness, codes of conduct, uniforms and timekeeping.


Build a High Performing Team


Actively facilitates self and team member development; constantly seeking new ways to build skills and competency; elevates the brand experience.


Responsibilities include:

  • Create an environment where employees thrive and look forward to coming to work.
  • Hold staff accountable for daily performance and following rules and regulations outlined in the Employee Handbook.
  • Train all caf associates on proper technique related to stocking items, receiving invoices, and completing waste logs.
  • Keep the caf aligned with TSI cleanliness standards.
  • Provide and/or offer continuing education for caf on new products.
  • Attend meetings to discuss caf performance and game-plan for future success.


Requirements

Achieve Measurable Results


Meets and exceeds company goals and metrics surrounding employees and members; never satisfied with the status quo.


Responsibilities include:

  • Create and implement proper caf associate schedule while staying within payroll budgets.
  • Promote new caf and club products.
  • Drive caf sales.
  • Report on daily dollar check-in to the GM.
  • Assist GM and other club leaders in ordering materials and supplies.

Required Skills and Experience


  • CPR and First Aid Training.
  • ServSafe Certified.
  • High School diploma or equivalent.
  • Managerial experience preferred.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Developing, designing, or creating new applications, ideas and relationships.
  • Developing and building teams.
  • Making decisions and problem solving.
  • Proficient with computers and software (Microsoft Word, Excel), Outlook Exchange. Experience with 10-line digital phone system. Ability to use cleaning tools, products, and chemical safely and effectively.
  • Ability to stay focused, efficient, and effective in managing multiple priorities.
  • Professional demeanor and ability to defuse emotional situations in a calm manner.
  • Strong interpersonal skills and good judgment.
  • Proven ability to work independently to achieve accomplishments.
  • Mature, energetic with a let s roll up our sleeves attitude.
  • Proven ability to effectively collaborate with internal team, cross-functional team and external parties.
  • Environmental requirements: limited temperature fluctuations, air conditioned environment.
  • Physical requirements include ability to stand for long periods of time, occasionally required to climb stairs, have normal eye/hand motor coordination and ability to grasp small objects, possess a normal extended reach, must be able to move about, lift up to 40lbs., and have the ability to determine colors and possess near perception. Able to operate the meat slicer.
