Project Overview

Problem approached

  •   Finding an appropriate job is demanding and stressful.
  •  The study shows that people with a disability have lower median incomes, are less likely to be employed.

Root Causes

  •  Many job postings do not include clear requirements regarding to disabilities.
  •  The study shows top three reasons for employer not hiring people with disabilities.
    1.  81.4% are worried about the cost of providing reasonable accommodations.
    2.  80.9% don’t know how to handle the needs of a worker with a disability.
    3.  80.2% are afraid they won’t be able to discipline or fire a worker with a disability for poor performance.

 

How our product solves problem - Scenario  

  •   Both employers and employees log into our website.
  •   Employers click "Employer" navigation bar and post their jobs via the provided template. They can also submit their document directly. After that, employers will get suggestions from AI job posting evaluators to make their job posting more inclusive and discrimination-free. After that, employers wait until they will wait until their job posting closing date.
  •  Employees click "Employee" navigation bar and submit a clear description of their disabilities via provided template. They can also submit their document directly.
  •   The machine learning model in our backend system classifies the list of the jobs suitable for the employees with certain disability and produce it as an output.
  •   Consequently, disabled people's employability increases as they easily get an organized list of jobs suitable or optimal for their condition. 

motivation  

The enthusiasm and empathy

  • We are young students who desire to make world more accessible and inclusive. Obtaining a satisfactory job is a crucial to have happy life. Hence, many people are preparing hard for it. However, it is more stressful and challenging for disabled people to catch satisfaction and desire when it comes to finding jobs. Our enthusiasm and empathy led us to create this AI-based product where disabled employees log into this website and get an organized list of jobs that are suitable or optimal for their physical or mental condition. On the other hand, employers could provide more inclusive and discrimination-free job positing via suggestion from AI job posting evaluator. Our product becomes solid and reliable bridge between disabled employee and employers. It reduces burdens on disabled employees for finding jobs while benefits employers to get government's supports for accommodation and employment cost.
